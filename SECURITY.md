# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Report suspected vulnerabilities by email to **security@darkrouter.com**.

Include enough detail to reproduce the issue: affected component, version or
commit, reproduction steps, and the impact you observed. If you have a proof of
concept, attach it.

Please report privately and do not disclose the issue publicly until we have
released a fix.

## What to expect

We acknowledge every report within **48 hours**. After triage we will keep you
informed of progress toward a fix and let you know when the issue is resolved.

## No bug bounty

We do not operate a bug bounty program and do not offer monetary rewards for
reports. We appreciate responsible disclosure and are glad to credit reporters
who wish to be acknowledged.
